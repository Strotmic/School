About this event

We are happy to invite you to the second edition of the Tech Challenge, organized by November Five! This year’s challenge will test your teamwork, problem-solving skills, and coding abilities, all while solving riddles, puzzles, and mysteries!

We’re keeping the details under wraps. But we promise an exciting and challenging experience that will keep you on your toes until the last minute.

How can you participate? Make a team of max 4 people. We encourage you to choose your team members carefully, as at least one of them needs to know programming, while the others should have a good amount of common sense, keep calm under pressure, and love problem-solving.
